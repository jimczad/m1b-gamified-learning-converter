Step 1: Get Your Questions Out of the Original Tool

Most quiz platforms do not offer a clean export that includes questions and correct answers. The goal of this step is to make sure the text is visible and selectable so it can be extracted accurately by the AI tool.

General Rule (Works for Most Tools)

  • Open the quiz or assessment while logged in
  • Turn on Show Answers or the Answer Key
  • Choose Print
  • When the browser’s print preview opens:
    • If the text is selectable, you can copy it directly from the preview
    • If you prefer, you may also Save as PDF and upload to the AI tool

Important Platform‑Specific Notes

Blooket

  • Open your question set
  • Turn on the Answer Key
  • Select Print
  • When the browser’s print preview opens:
    • Do NOT save the PDF — Blooket exports quizzes as images
    • Copy the questions and answers directly from the print preview
  • Paste the copied text into the AI Converter (Step 2)

Kahoot, Gimkit

  • Make sure Show Answers is turned on before printing
  • You may either copy from print preview or save as a PDF
Quizlet
  • If you own the set: use Export → Copy Text
  • If not: Print → copy from preview or save as PDF
    • Wayground
  • Switch to Worksheet view
  • Turn on the Answer Key
  • Print → copy from preview or download as PDF

    • Once you have the questions and answers copied or saved, continue to Step 2.
